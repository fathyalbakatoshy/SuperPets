Welcome to the Super Pet

Super Pet is the first primary care veterinary clinic and pet shop in Alexandria. We are dedicated to providing the highest quality medical care and products for your beloved pets. Our team of experienced veterinarians and caring staff offer professional services to keep your furry, feathered or scaly friends happy and healthy.

In addition to our veterinary clinic, our pet shop boasts an impressive variety of pet supplies, food, toys, and accessories. We carry everything you need to pamper and care for your pets, from the everyday essentials to the latest innovative products on the market.

At Super Pet, we understand that your pets are part of your family. That's why we strive to create a welcoming, compassionate environment where you and your pets always feel at home. Visit us today and discover the Super Pet difference!
